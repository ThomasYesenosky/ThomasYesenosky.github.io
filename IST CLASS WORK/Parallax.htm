Bears

Thank you for stopping by and learning more about bears! Bears are a carnivoran mammal from the family Ursidae. There are sadly only 8 species of bear in existence, but they are very widespread throughout the Northern Hemisphere and even a little bit in the southern hemisphere. The commone characteristics of this predators are very large and tough bodies with stocky legs, long snouts, small rounded ears , shaggy hair, plantigrade paws with 5 claws, and very short tails. Although they are almost all mostly carnivors, the panda is actually mostly a herbivor, starkly different than its counter part the polar bear which is almost soley carnivorous.
